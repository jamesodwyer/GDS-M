# Patterns

## Links
- All text links are primary blue with underline.

## Buttons
- Variants: primary, secondary, tertiary, ghost.
- Border radius: 4px on all buttons.
- Spacing between buttons: 8px both vertically and horizontally.

## Typography
- Follow foundations typography sizes.
- Headings: uppercase, black font weight.
- On black backgrounds: font colour is white with a primary blue underline. Underline length matches the text and is 4px high.

## Cards
- White cards must sit on a Lunar background.
- Elevation: level 1.
- Border radius: 0px.
